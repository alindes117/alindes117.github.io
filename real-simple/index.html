CURVES OF STEEL

Leave spoon-bending tricks to psychics and magicians. For more amazing feat, breathe new life into mismatched and old flatware by turning it into-ta-da!-useful hooks.

do it yourself: 3 steps

Silverware that won't see a table anytime soon finds a new purpose on a wall.

1. DRILL

Use a titanium or cobalt drill bit (or another type for drilling metal) to bore a small hole in the handle of the utensil.

2. BEND

Thin silver is malleable, and a heat gun on a hair dryer makes forming U-shapes even easier.

3. NAIL

Attach the hooks to the wall with a hammer and a nail. Take care to hang only lightweight items from the hooks.
