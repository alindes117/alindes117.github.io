ABOUT US

Wreft transforms fitness tracking and health monitoring into a removable, non-distracting, customizable tech tattoo. We wanted a product that would track more than just your heart rate or the number of steps you take.

Getting up and going for a run is great, but how about keeping track of your water and nutrition intake, sugar levels, or blood pressure without the need of an extra device? We hope Wreft's advanced tracking system will allow a more convenient way for people to monitor their health.

The tattoo will glow a certain color to indicate what your body is lacking:

  • Hydration Level

    Our body consists of about 60% water and in order to stay hydrated our need to drink at least 8 glasses of water.

  • Nutrition Intake

    In addition to proteins and carbohydrates, our body needs to ingest fibres, vitamins, and minerals.

  • Daily Exercise

    To maintain a healthy body, we need to do any type of physical activity for at least 30 minutes a day.

THE APP

Monitor. Compete. Improve.

The app displays your hydration level, nutrition intake, and exercises progress. You can view the data by months, weeks, days, and even hourly. Wreft will help make sure you're healthy and energized by pushing notifications to remind you when you're lacking something.

Challenge yourself or your friends to win badges and trophies. See who can eat the healthiest or exercise the most. This is a great way to motivate each other.

In addition, you can customize Wreft to monitor steps, distance, biking distance, adreline levels, energy levels, and more!
